About Us





We are a committee formed to manage the activities of both the Diploma in Infocomm Security Management Special Interest Group (DISM SIG) and the ISACA Singapore Polytechnic Student Group (ISACA SP), with advice from the lecturers of DISM.


Our Mission



Course Bonding

Promote course cohesion through hosting events and activities for all DISM students to participate and interact with each other.

Management

Unified management of the different DISM student chapters to better serve the interest of the course and students.
